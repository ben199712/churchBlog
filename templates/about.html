Our Story

Founded in 1985, Grace Seventh-day Adventist Church has been a beacon of hope and faith in our community for nearly four decades.

What began as a small group of believers meeting in a community center has grown into a vibrant congregation of over 500 members. Our church family represents diverse backgrounds, ages, and cultures, united by our shared faith in Jesus Christ and commitment to the Seventh-day Adventist message.

Throughout our history, we have remained steadfast in our mission to share God's love, serve our community, and prepare hearts for Christ's soon return. We believe in the power of fellowship, the importance of Sabbath worship, and the transformative impact of living according to biblical principles.

What We Believe

As Seventh-day Adventists, we hold to the fundamental beliefs that guide our faith and practice.

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God and our ultimate authority for faith and practice.

The Sabbath

We observe the seventh-day Sabbath as a holy day of rest and worship, following God's commandment.

Salvation

We believe salvation comes through faith in Jesus Christ alone, by God's grace.

Second Coming

We eagerly await the soon return of Jesus Christ to establish His eternal kingdom.

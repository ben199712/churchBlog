Pastor Robert Johnson

Senior Pastor

Pastor Robert Johnson serves at Grace Seventh-day Adventist Church as the Senior Pastor. A dedicated servant of God with over 15 years of ministry experience, his passion for biblical teaching is only surpassed by his deep love for God and His people. He is married to Sarah Johnson, and together, they have a thriving family dedicated to serving the Lord.

"Our mission is to establish the kingdom of God here on earth through faithful service and unwavering commitment to His word."
